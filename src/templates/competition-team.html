Forge Champions

Our Competition Team is for dedicated athletes who want to test their skills in tournaments and competitions. Through intensive training, advanced techniques, and mental preparation, we develop complete competitors.

Joining the Competition Team

Minimum Experience

At least 6 months of consistent training in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Wrestling, or Kickboxing. Demonstrated technical proficiency and understanding of fundamentals.

Commitment Level

Attend team training sessions 3-4 times per week. Participate in regular class instruction. Commitment to compete at least twice per year.

Instructor Approval

Invitation by head instructor based on technical skill, attitude, and commitment to training. Interview process to ensure team fit and goals alignment.

Physical Fitness

Good physical condition with ability to handle intensive training. Medical clearance for competition participation required.
