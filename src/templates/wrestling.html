  • Hip tosses and throws
  • Sprawls and takedown defense

  • Escapes and reversals
  • Wrestling-specific conditioning

  • Mental toughness and resilience
  • Improved balance and coordination
  • Confidence in standing exchanges

Wrestling Styles We Teach

Freestyle Wrestling

Olympic style wrestling that allows attacks to the legs and upper body. Emphasizes explosive takedowns and dynamic movements.

Folkstyle Wrestling

American collegiate wrestling style focusing on control, escapes, and riding time. Builds incredible conditioning and mental toughness.

No-Gi Grappling

Wrestling techniques adapted for submission grappling without the gi. Perfect for MMA and modern grappling competitions.

Takedowns for Self-Defense

Practical wrestling techniques for real-world situations. Focus on simple, effective takedowns and maintaining control.

What to Expect

Intensity Level

Wrestling classes are physically demanding and will push your conditioning. We scale intensity based on experience level, but expect to work hard and sweat.

What to Wear

Wear fitted athletic clothes - compression shorts/leggings and a fitted t-shirt or rashguard. Wrestling shoes are recommended but not required for beginners.

Safety First

We emphasize proper technique and controlled sparring. Our instructors ensure a safe learning environment while maintaining the authentic intensity of wrestling.

Mental Game

Wrestling builds incredible mental toughness. You'll develop the ability to push through fatigue and maintain focus under pressure - skills that transfer to all areas of life.
