Why We Exist

At Oregon Jiu Jitsu Lab, our goal is simple: help beginners and advanced grapplers build skills in the most efficient way possible.

Our lineage goes back to Master Rigan Machado through Professor Chris Haueter, one of the first black belts in the United States. We honor that tradition while teaching American Jiu Jitsu — a style shaped by Brazilian roots and influenced by Judo and Wrestling, built for the way athletes train today.
