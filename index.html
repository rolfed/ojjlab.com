Programs

American Jiu Jitsu

Jiu Jitsu is rooted in tradition, but it has evolved.

Our lineage goes back to Master Rigan Machado through Professor Chris Haueter, one of the first black belts in the United States. At OJJ Lab, we call our style American Jiu Jitsu because we build on Brazilian roots while also learning from Judo and Wrestling. We honor where Jiu Jitsu began, but we embrace how American practitioners continue to push it forward.

Kids Jiu Jitsu (Ages 4+)

Confidence starts young.

We believe in setting kids up for success on and off the mats. Our classes build focus, give immediate feedback, and encourage steady growth. Kids are challenged physically and mentally, and we also guide them to grow as leaders.
