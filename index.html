Programs

American Jiu Jitsu

Jiu Jitsu is rooted in tradition, but it has evolved.

Our lineage goes back to Master Rigan Machado through Professor Chris Haueter, one of the first black belts in the United States. At OJJ Lab, we call our style American Jiu Jitsu because we build on Brazilian roots while also learning from Judo and Wrestling. We honor where Jiu Jitsu began, but we embrace how American practitioners continue to push it forward.

Kids Jiu Jitsu (Ages 4+)

Confidence starts young.

We believe in setting kids up for success on and off the mats. Our classes build focus, give immediate feedback, and encourage steady growth. Kids are challenged physically and mentally, and we also guide them to grow as leaders.

Learn More

Adult Jiu Jitsu

Train hard. Grow stronger.

Whether you’re stepping onto the mats for the first time or looking to sharpen your skills, our adult program balances technique, fitness, and strategy. Jiu Jitsu is a practice that builds resilience, community, and strength at every stage of life.

Learn More

Competition Team

For those who want to test themselves.

We believe in teaching systems, not just one-off moves. Our competition program gives athletes advanced techniques, focused coaching, and training partners who push them to improve. It is built for serious practitioners who want to take their skills to the next level.

Learn More

Kickboxing

Our kickboxing program builds striking skills for fitness, self-defense, and competition. Classes focus on proper technique, conditioning, and confidence. Training is open to beginners and experienced athletes alike.
