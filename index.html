Programs

American Jiu Jitsu

A martial art rooted in tradition, evolved for today.

Our lineage traces back to Master Rigan Machado through Coach Chris Haueter, one of the original black belts in the United States. But Jiu Jitsu has grown far beyond its beginnings.

At OJJ Lab, we call our style American Jiu Jitsu — shaped by Brazilian roots, but influenced by Judo, and Wrestling. We honor our heritage while embracing the evolution led by American practictioners pushing the art forward.
